About KLOSTER BERLIN

Embarking on a Journey through Berlin's Nightlife

We invite you to embark on a journey through the nightlife of Berlin. Imagine the energy created by the street lamps illuminating the night sky; this is exactly what KLOSTER BERLIN embodies.

The heart of our project lies in Berlin, a metropolis pulsating with unique nightlife. We initiated this project to understand, absorb, and witness the reflections of the immense energy that emerges. Our focus is on the diversity of the nightlife of individuals engaged in the party culture, their preferred styles of clothing, and the colors of their music choices.

In this vibrant hub filled with music, art, and character, Berlin reveals its multifaceted soul. The size and diversity of this city are vast, possibly beyond what each of us can fully experience. However, we may not be able to present the entirety of this picture to you. Yet, we believe that through a whisper, we offer an energy that can bring us and you closer together.

Who knows, through the stories, music, and people we discover in this project, we might come closer to each other. Sharing the energy, music, and character of Berlin actually means coming a bit closer to one another. As KLOSTER BERLIN, we invite all of you to this journey filled with energy!

Remember, perhaps a whisper can bring us even closer to each other...



About the Creators

Osman Özel is a multidisciplinary artist based in Berlin. With roots in a family of filmmakers, Osman has developed his skills as a photographer and filmmaker and has gained international recognition for his work. His photographs, video clips, and 30 short films in the docudrama style have reached a wide audience. With a drive to constantly improve and tell stories from different cultural perspectives, Osman is represented as an artist by Shotview Artist Management. He is on the hunt for hidden stories and experiences that have been forgotten around the corner. Believing that memories connect us to the future, when he is not behind the camera, Osman explores new forms of artistic expression, including art installations that combine dance and other disciplines to create immersive experiences for audiences. He is the creative director of KLOSTER BERLIN.
